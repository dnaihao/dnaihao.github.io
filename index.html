I am interested in the three aspects of NLP. First, using NLP tools to understand the way human beings perceive the world, and build NLP models that can perceive the world better by studying how human beings perceive the world. Second, building NLP applications that can help research and real-life applications. Third, using NLP tools to study social problems systematically.
